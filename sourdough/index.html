MJ's Josey Baker Country Loaf

This is my adaptation of the popular recipe from Josey Baker Bread. This recipe yields two loaves.

Steps, In General

  1. Feeding the starter
  2. Feeding the preferment
  3. Mixing
  4. Bulk fermentation
  5. Shaping
  6. Cold fermentation
  7. Baking

Ingredients

  • Rye starter
  • Rye flour (for starter)
  • 500g whole wheat flour + extra (for dusting)
  • 500g bread flour
    • OR 480g all purpose flour + 20g vital wheat gluten
  • 20 g salt
  • Rice flour (for dusting)

Equipment

  • 1 clear container (16+ oz, for starter)
  • 1 clear container (24+ oz, for preferment)
  • 2 woven baskets
  • Plastic or ziplock bags
  • Parchment paper
  • Lame or razor blade
  • Dutch oven with lid
